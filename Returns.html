Return Policy

We want you to love your FurniHome purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, you can request a return within 7 days of delivery. Our customer-centric approach ensures a smooth return experience with minimal effort on your part.

All returns are subject to inspection upon receipt. Once approved, your refund will be processed promptly.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Item must be unused and in original packaging with all tags attached
  • Original invoice or proof of purchase must be provided
  • Customized or made-to-order items are non-returnable
  • Minor color variations due to screen differences are not considered defects
  • Assembly must not have been attempted for furniture items
  • Return request must be initiated within 7 days of delivery

How to Return

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

Request Return

Log into your account and raise a return request or contact our support team for assistance.

Pickup Scheduled

We arrange a convenient pickup within 2–4 business days at your preferred location.

Quality Check

Product is thoroughly inspected at our warehouse to ensure return conditions are met.

Refund Processed

Refund issued to your original payment method within 7–10 business days after approval.

Refunds

Refunds are processed to the original payment method used during purchase. Shipping charges (if any) are non-refundable unless the item is defective or damaged.

Refund Timelines:
• Credit/Debit Cards: 7-10 business days
• PayPal: 3-5 business days
• Bank Transfer: 10-14 business days

Damaged or Defective Items

Important: Please report damaged or defective items within 48 hours of delivery with clear photos or videos showing the issue.

We will arrange for a replacement or refund immediately upon verification.

Need Help With a Return?

Our dedicated support team is available 7 days a week to assist you with any questions or concerns about returns and refunds.
