How to Measure Your Space

Accurate measurements are crucial for selecting furniture that fits perfectly in your room. Follow this step-by-step guide to ensure you have all the dimensions you need.

1

Measure Room Dimensions

Measure length, width, and height of the room. Note the positions of doors, windows, and electrical outlets.

2

Consider Clearance Space

Leave at least 60–75 cm for walking areas and 90–100 cm for door openings and drawers.

3

Visualize Placement

Use masking tape on the floor or a room planning app to visualize furniture placement before purchasing.

Expert Size Tips

Professional advice to help you make the best furniture decisions for your space.

Measure Twice, Buy Once

Always double-check measurements of doorways, hallways, and staircases to ensure furniture can be delivered.

Clearance Space

Leave adequate space for doors to open fully, drawers to extend, and people to move comfortably around furniture.

Visualize with Tape

Use masking tape on the floor to outline furniture dimensions and experiment with different layouts.
