Warranty Overview

All FurniHome products come with a comprehensive limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects under normal residential use. Our warranty reflects our confidence in the quality and durability of every piece we create.

The warranty period begins from the date of delivery and is valid for the original purchaser only. Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

What's Covered vs. What's Not

Understanding the scope of your warranty coverage helps set proper expectations.

Covered

  • Manufacturing defects: Cracks, structural issues, or faulty craftsmanship
  • Material defects: Issues with raw materials, veneers, or laminates
  • Workmanship errors: Improper assembly, finishing, or construction
  • Hardware failures: Manufacturer defects in hinges, handles, or mechanisms
  • Structural integrity: Frame or support system failures under normal use

Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear: Fading, scratches, or minor dents from regular use
  • Improper use or handling: Damage from misuse, overloading, or accidents
  • Environmental damage: Exposure to moisture, heat, sunlight, or pests
  • Unauthorized modifications: Repairs or alterations by non-authorized personnel
  • Commercial use: Furniture used in commercial or rental properties

How to File a Warranty Claim

Follow these simple steps to submit a warranty claim. Our team will guide you through the process.

1

Gather Information

Collect your order details, photos of the issue, and proof of purchase.

2

Contact Support

Reach out via phone, email, or our online portal with your information.

3

Evaluation

Our warranty team reviews your claim within 2-3 business days.

4

Resolution

Approved claims receive repair, replacement, or credit as appropriate.

Important Notice: Warranty service does not include cash refunds. Repair or replacement will be provided at the company's discretion based on the nature of the defect. Warranty claims must be submitted within 30 days of discovering the issue. Extended warranty options are available for purchase at checkout.
