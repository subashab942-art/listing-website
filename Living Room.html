Living Room Arrangement Tips

Expert advice to help you create the perfect living space

Create Conversation Areas

Arrange seating to face each other, allowing for comfortable conversation. Leave 1-1.5 meters between seating pieces.

Layer Your Lighting

Combine overhead lights with floor lamps, table lamps, and accent lighting to create depth and ambiance.

Add Textures & Layers

Mix different textures through rugs, cushions, throws, and materials to add warmth and visual interest.

Consider Traffic Flow

Leave clear pathways (at least 90cm wide) for easy movement through the room without disrupting seating areas.
