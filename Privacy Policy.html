At FurniHome, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

This policy applies to all users in India and complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide our services:

Personal Information

  • Name & Contact Details: Full name, email address, phone number
  • Address: Billing and shipping addresses
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details (processed securely via PCI-compliant gateways)
  • Account Details: Username, password (encrypted), preferences

Usage & Technical Data

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP (for regional offers)
  • Cookies: Session data, preferences, analytics cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide better services:

  • Order Processing: To process and deliver your orders efficiently
  • Customer Support: To provide personalized assistance and after-sales service
  • Improvement: To enhance our website, products, and user experience
  • Communication: To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and promotional offers (opt-out available)
  • Security: To prevent fraud and ensure secure transactions
  • Analytics: To analyze website performance and user behavior

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential, performance, and marketing cookies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our Cookie Settings page.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality (shopping cart, user login)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics (anonymized data for improvement)
  • Marketing Cookies: Facebook Pixel, retargeting ads (with consent)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

4. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data:

  • Encryption: 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • PCI Compliance: Secure payment processing through certified gateways
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in secure AWS India data centers
  • Access Control: Employee access limited by role-based permissions

5. Third-Party Services

We share information only when necessary with trusted partners:

  • Payment Gateways: Razorpay, PayU (PCI compliant)
  • Shipping Partners: Delhivery, Blue Dart (delivery details only)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymized data)
  • Marketing: Mailchimp, Facebook (with consent)
  • Customer Support: Zendesk, Freshdesk

6. Your Rights (DPDP Act)

As per Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request data removal (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-out: Stop marketing communications anytime
  • Grievance: File complaints with our Data Protection Officer
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format

To exercise your rights, email dpo@furnihome.com or use our Privacy Dashboard.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with new "Last Updated" date
  • Announced via email (for registered users)
  • Displayed prominently on our website for 30 days
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
